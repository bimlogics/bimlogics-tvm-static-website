PROJECTS

Resort Project:

Our mandate for this project was to generate the BIM model from the AutoCAD input data, to aid the MEPF services. Using Revit, Navisworks and dynamo scripts, we created 3D models at LOD 400 level.
Office Project:

Our scope of work for this project include BIM coordination between the architectural, structural and MEPF services. We took advantage of 5D to extract quantities from the BIM model, to aid the site team for material management.
Apartment Project:

Our mandate for this residential tower was to coordinate the BIM model for its MEP services. Coordinate with the sub-contractors, we updated the model using Revit, Autocad and Navisworks, based on our meetings.

Commercial Project:

We worked in this project from the design stage onwards. We designed and created the BIM model of Electrical and Plumbing services from LOD 100 to 400 level. We provided on-site coordination also for this project, to support the site engineering team.
